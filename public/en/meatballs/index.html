Meatballs

beef

Utensils List

  • Wooden spoon
  • Knife & chopping board

Ingredients (4 servings)

  • 500g beef mince
  • 1 medium onion, finely diced
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 egg
  • 40g breadcrumbs
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • For the sauce:
    • 1 can (400g) chopped tomatoes
    • 1 tbsp tomato paste
    • Salt & pepper to taste

Meal Prep Steps

  1. Finely dice the onion and mince the garlic.
  2. Combine mince, onion, garlic, egg, breadcrumbs, parsley, oregano, paprika, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  3. Mix gently until just combined (do not overwork).
  4. Roll into 12–16 meatballs.

Cooking Instructions

Option 1 — Pan-fried (most flavourful)

  1. Heat olive oil in a frying pan over medium heat.
  2. Brown the meatballs on all sides (about 6–8 minutes).
  3. Remove excess oil if needed.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes and tomato paste.
  5. Simmer for 20 minutes until sauce thickens and meatballs are cooked through.

Option 2 — Oven-baked

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C.
  2. Place meatballs on a lined baking tray.
  3. Bake 18–20 minutes until browned.
  4. Transfer to a saucepan with tomatoes + tomato paste, and simmer 10 minutes.

Storage & Reheating Instructions

  • Fridge: Store in an airtight container for up to 3 days.
  • Freezer: Freeze meatballs with sauce for up to 3 months.
  • Reheat: Microwave 2–3 minutes or simmer on the hob until hot throughout.
