## Ingredients (Serves 2)

- 2 chicken breast fillets (about 250–300 g total)  
- 2 medium potatoes, peeled and diced  
- 2 medium carrots, diced  
- 2 celery sticks, sliced  
- 1 small onion, finely chopped  
- 1 leek, sliced  
- 2 cloves garlic, minced  
- 1 tbsp olive oil  
- 1 litre chicken stock (made with stock cube)  
- 1 bay leaf  
- ½ tsp dried thyme  
- Salt and black pepper, to taste  
- Fresh parsley, chopped (to garnish)  

---

## Meal Prep Steps

1. Peel and dice the potatoes; keep them in cold water until needed.
2. Wash, peel, and chop all other vegetables.
3. Trim chicken breasts and cut into bite-sized pieces.
4. Prepare chicken stock with hot water if using a cube.

---

## Cooking Instructions

1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat.
2. Add onion, leek, carrots, celery, and potatoes. Cook for 5–7 minutes, stirring occasionally.
3. Stir in garlic and cook for 30 seconds until fragrant.
4. Add chicken pieces and cook until lightly sealed.
5. Pour in the chicken stock, then add bay leaf and thyme.
6. Bring to a gentle boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 25 minutes, until potatoes are tender.
7. Season with salt and black pepper to taste.
8. Remove bay leaf and garnish with fresh parsley before serving.

---

## Storage & Reheating Instructions

- **Storage:** Cool completely and store in an airtight container in the fridge for up to 3 days.
- **Freezing:** Freeze for up to 2 months.
- **Reheating:** Reheat gently on the hob or in the microwave until piping hot.
