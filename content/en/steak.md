## Ingredients (Serves 1)

- 1 beef steak (250–300 g, 2–3 cm thick)
- 1 tsp olive or rapeseed oil
- ½ tsp sea salt
- ¼ tsp black pepper
- ½ tsp garlic powder (optional)
- ½ tsp smoked paprika (optional)
- 10 g butter (optional)

---

## Meal Prep Steps

1. Remove steak from the fridge 20–30 minutes before cooking.
2. Pat dry thoroughly with kitchen paper.
3. Preheat air fryer to **200°C** for 3–5 minutes.
4. Lightly oil and season both sides of the steak.

---

## Cooking Instructions

1. Place steak in the air fryer basket in a single layer.
2. Cook at **200°C**, turning halfway:
   - Rare: 6–7 min (50°C internal)
   - Medium-rare: 8–9 min (55–57°C)
   - Medium: 10–11 min (60–63°C)
   - Well-done: 12–14 min (68°C+)
3. Remove steak and rest for **5 minutes**.
4. Finish with butter or herbs if desired.

---

## Storage & Reheating Instructions

- Store leftovers in an airtight container in the fridge for up to **2 days**.
- Reheat in the air fryer at **160°C for 3–4 minutes**.
- Avoid microwaving to prevent toughness.
