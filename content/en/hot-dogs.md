## Ingredients (Serves 4)
- 8 beef hot dogs  
- 8 hot dog buns  
- 1 small onion, finely chopped  
- 1 tomato, diced  
- 2 gherkins, sliced  
- Ketchup, to taste  
- Mustard, to taste  
- Mayonnaise (optional)  
- 1 tsp oil or butter  

---

## Meal Prep Steps
1. Finely chop the onion and dice the tomato.  
2. Slice the gherkins.  
3. Open the buns and set aside.  

---

## Cooking Instructions
1. Heat a saucepan with water and bring to a gentle simmer, or heat a frying pan with a little oil.  
2. Cook the hot dogs for 5â€“7 minutes until heated through (simmering) or lightly browned (pan).  
3. Warm the buns briefly in a dry pan or oven.  
4. Place hot dogs into buns.  
5. Top with onion, tomato, gherkins, and sauces of choice.  

---

## Storage & Reheating Instructions
- Store cooked hot dogs in an airtight container in the fridge for up to 2 days.  
- Reheat gently in a pan or microwave until hot.  
- Assemble buns fresh for best texture.
