# Banana Bread

## Grocery List

### Produce
- 3 ripe bananas

### Pantry
- Plain flour  
- Granulated sugar  
- Baking powder  
- Baking soda  
- Vegetable oil (or melted butter)  
- Vanilla extract  

### Spices
- Ground cinnamon  
- Salt  

### Proteins
- Eggs  

### Other
- Baking parchment (optional)

---

## Utensils List
- Mixing bowls  
- Fork or masher  
- Whisk or spoon  
- Loaf tin (1 lb or 2 lb)  
- Oven or air fryer  
- Measuring cups/spoons  

---

## Ingredients (Makes 1 loaf)
- 3 ripe bananas, mashed  
- 200 g plain flour  
- 120 g sugar  
- 1 tsp baking powder  
- ½ tsp baking soda  
- ½ tsp salt  
- 1 tsp cinnamon  
- 2 eggs  
- 80 ml vegetable oil (or 75 g melted butter)  
- 1 tsp vanilla extract  

---

## Meal Prep Steps
1. Line the loaf tin with parchment or lightly grease it.  
2. Mash the bananas until smooth.  
3. Pre-measure dry ingredients to speed up mixing.  

---

## Cooking Instructions

### Oven Method
1. Preheat oven to **175°C**.  
2. In a bowl, mix mashed bananas, eggs, oil, and vanilla.  
3. In another bowl, combine flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, salt, and cinnamon.  
4. Fold dry ingredients into wet until just combined—do not overmix.  
5. Pour batter into loaf tin.  
6. Bake for **50–60 minutes**, or until a skewer comes out clean.  
7. Cool in the tin for 10 minutes, then transfer to a rack.

### Air Fryer Method
1. Preheat air fryer to **150°C**.  
2. Prepare batter as above.  
3. Pour into a small loaf tin that fits the basket.  
4. Air fry for **35–45 minutes**. Check after 30 minutes.  
5. Let cool before slicing.

---

## Storage & Reheating Instructions
- **Room temperature:** Store wrapped for up to 3 days.  
- **Fridge:** Keeps 5–7 days in an airtight container.  
- **Freeze:** Slice and freeze up to 3 months; thaw at room temperature.  
- **Reheat:** Warm slices in microwave for 10–15 seconds or toast lightly.
